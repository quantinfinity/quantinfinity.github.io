date: 2024-12-01

**QuantRUC lab** successfully cohosted the Renmin University Fintech Hackathon 2024. The hackathon had **198 teams** participating and totally **1916 model submissions**. This event brought together talented students and professionals to innovate and create solutions in the field of finance modeling by using machine learning algorithms and Artificial Intelligence. Participants engaged in collaborative problem-solving, leveraging cutting-edge technologies to address real-world challenges in fintech.
